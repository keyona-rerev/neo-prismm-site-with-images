About Prismm

Built by bankers.
For banks.

Prismm was founded on a simple observation: banks have sophisticated infrastructure for every stage of the account lifecycle, except the end.

The origin

A problem that shouldn't still exist

After 25+ years in regulated banking and financial technology, Prismm founder Martha Underwood saw the same failure repeated at every institution she worked with: when a customer died, the bank had no system, no process, and no infrastructure to manage what happened next.

Deposits left. Beneficiaries were lost. Operations teams spent months on manual processes. And the institution bore the cost, not just in lost revenue, but in broken relationships with the families of their most loyal customers.

Prismm was founded to build the infrastructure that should have existed decades ago. Not a tool. Not just storage. Infrastructure, the same way payments rails are infrastructure for money movement.
