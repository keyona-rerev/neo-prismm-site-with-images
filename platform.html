The platform

Prismm Anchor

The core estate orchestration platform that embeds into your bank's existing infrastructure at the point of account origination, not as a bolt-on, but as foundational infrastructure.

Core capabilities

Three capabilities that define estate infrastructure

Triggered access & delegation

Automatically activates designated contacts and permissions upon a verified life event. Replaces manual notification with structured, compliant handoffs.

Account transition automation

Replaces months of manual, paper-driven processes with structured digital workflows that reduce administrative burden, accelerate resolution, and create auditable compliance trails.

Intergenerational retention

Engages beneficiaries and next-of-kin before a death event occurs. Creates new account relationships and deposit retention pathways, turning estate readiness into a growth engine.

Additional platform benefits

  • Life event verification engine: Validates death certificates, incapacitation documents, and legal instruments through automated, compliance-ready workflows.
  • Core banking integration: Embeds at the point of account origination via core banking platforms. Estate readiness becomes part of account setup.
  • AI-powered estate readiness (Prismm Lens): Analyzes account holder data at origination to surface estate readiness gaps and prioritize outreach.
