Why Prismm

Not just storage. Not a tool.
Infrastructure.

The distinction matters. Here's why Prismm exists in a category of its own and why no existing solution addresses what banks actually need.

Solution 01

Deposit retention at death

When an account holder dies, deposits don't stay. They scatter to other institutions, to attorneys, to friction and delay. Prismm creates structured pathways that keep estate-linked assets at your institution.

By capturing beneficiary designations and estate readiness data at account origination, Prismm ensures that when a life event occurs, there's a clear, compliant path for assets to remain with your institution.

Solution 02

Account transition automation

The average manual account transition takes 6 to 18 months of phone calls, paper forms, legal review, and compliance checks. Prismm replaces this with structured digital workflows.

From life event verification through final account transition, Prismm Anchor automates each step with compliance-ready audit trails. Your operations team handles in weeks what currently takes months.

Solution 03

Intergenerational relationship capture

When a customer dies, their beneficiaries are your next customers, if you can reach them. Today, most institutions lose these relationships entirely.

Prismm captures beneficiary and next-of-kin data at account origination, creating a direct pathway to the next generation. When a life event occurs, these connections are already established, turning account transitions into acquisition opportunities.
